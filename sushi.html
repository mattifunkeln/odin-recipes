Sushi

Description

Sushi (すし, 寿司, 鮨, 鮓) is a traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice (鮨飯, sushi-meshi). It is typically seasoned with sugar and salt, and combined with a variety of ingredients (ねた, neta), such as seafood, vegetables, or meat

Sushi is sometimes confused with sashimi, a dish that consists of thinly sliced raw fish or occasionally meat, without sushi rice.

Ingredients

  • fish
  • vinegared rice
    • #
  • nori
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Place your nori sheet longest edge at the bottom
  2. Add a thin layer of rice
  3. Add a filling of your choice
  4. Roll and cut into pieces
